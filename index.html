LAM NGUYEN

PHOTOGRAPHER | VIDEOGRAPHER

My name is Lam Nguyen. I am from Vietnam and currently studying in Toronto, Canada. I am also working as a freelance photographer. I enjoy taking photos that capture real moments, emotions, and movement. I like to capture moments that people might not notice at first but still tell a story. Living in Toronto has helped me learn new things and improve my photography skills. I try to combine what I learn with my own style to create images that feel natural and meaningful. I am always open to new opportunities and collaborations. Through my work, I hope to share my perspective and continue growing as a photographer.
